Most small business owners know AI is important. But when you sit down to figure out where to start, the options are overwhelming — and most of the advice online is written for tech companies, not for someone running a restaurant, a landscaping crew, or a boutique.

So here's the short list. Five tools. Real use cases. No jargon.

## 1. ChatGPT (or Claude) — Your New $20/Month Employee

Think of ChatGPT as a generalist assistant who never sleeps. Small business owners are using it to:

- Write customer emails and follow-ups in seconds
- Create social media captions for the week in one sitting
- Draft job postings, contracts, and SOPs
- Answer "how do I do X" questions about their business

The key is being specific in how you ask. Instead of "write me an email," say "write a friendly follow-up email to a customer who hasn't responded in a week. I run a landscaping company. Keep it short and professional."

**Cost:** Free tier available. $20/month for full access.

## 2. Zapier — Connect Your Apps Without Code

Every business has repetitive tasks that happen between apps. Someone fills out a form → you manually enter it into a spreadsheet → you send a confirmation email. Zapier automates that entire chain.

Real examples:
- New contact form submission → automatically added to your CRM + you get a text
- New invoice paid → automatically sends a thank-you email
- New Google review → automatically posts to your Slack

**Cost:** Free tier handles most small business needs.

## 3. Notion AI — Your Business Brain

Notion is a workspace where you can store everything — notes, SOPs, client info, team wikis. Notion AI sits on top and lets you:

- Search across everything you've ever written
- Summarize long documents instantly
- Draft new content based on what's already in your workspace

**Cost:** Notion free tier + AI add-on is ~$10/month.

## 4. Otter.ai — Never Take Meeting Notes Again

Every meeting you have gets transcribed automatically. Otter records, transcribes, and summarizes your calls in real time. Review a 45-minute meeting in 3 minutes.

Small business use case: Run every client discovery call through Otter. After the call, you have a full transcript and summary. No more "wait, what did they say they needed?"

**Cost:** Free tier includes 300 minutes/month.

## 5. Canva AI — Professional Visuals Without a Designer

Canva has built AI tools directly into its design platform. You can:

- Generate images and graphics from a text prompt
- Resize designs instantly for every platform
- Use "Magic Write" to generate ad copy inside your design

For small businesses spending money on a graphic designer for basic social posts — Canva AI eliminates that cost.

**Cost:** Free tier is robust. Pro is $13/month.
